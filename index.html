About Cream & Flutter

Cream & Flutter is Mumbai's premier destination for gourmet, eggless bakery and confectionery, founded by Puja Jain and Chef Abdul Ansari. We specialize in fresh, handcrafted cakes, desserts, and savory treats, with a focus on quality, creativity, and customer delight. Discover more about our story, team, and philosophy on our dedicated About Us page.
