Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?
Our cafe and bakery are open from 10.00 am to 11.00 pm, every day.
Do you offer takeout and delivery?
Yes, we offer takeout and delivery through our partners Swiggy & Zomato.
Do you have sugar-free, gluten-free, or vegan options?
Yes, we have sugar-free, gluten-free, Jain, and vegan options available.
How do I place an order?
You can place an order in-person, through our website, email, on call (Limited time), Swiggy and Zomato.
Do you offer custom cake designs?
Yes, we can create custom cakes for special occasions. Contact us for details!
How can I contact you?
You can reach us at info@creamandflutter.in or call +91-8291668767.
