About MBJ GLOBAL

Founded in 2007, MBJ GLOBAL is a diversified conglomerate active in agro-industry, minerals, technology, eco-friendly products, and jewelry. We specialize in flour milling, food processing, mineral extraction, sustainable technologies, and diamond jewelry manufacturing. Our advanced facilities provide quality and innovation with solutions like battery regeneration, waste-to-energy, purified water, and hydroponics. Expanding across India and globally, we create jobs, strengthen supply chains, and support communities with a strong commitment to sustainability and customer satisfaction.
